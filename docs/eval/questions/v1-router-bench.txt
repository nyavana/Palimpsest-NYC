# Ten questions used in §13.6 router cost analysis.
# The same 10 prompts are run twice — once with the default free Gemma router
# config, once with a paid OpenAI config — so we can compare $/walk and
# latency holding the prompt distribution constant. Mix of single-place,
# multi-place, theme, and geographic queries to exercise both `standard` and
# `complex` routes.
Tell me about the Cathedral of Saint John the Divine.
What is Grant's Tomb and why is it in this neighborhood?
Plan a walking tour of three Columbia University landmarks.
Describe the architecture of Riverside Church.
What can I see if I walk from Low Plaza to Morningside Park?
Show me art-history landmarks in Morningside Heights.
What's the history of the Apollo Theater? (note: out of v1 scope)
Plan a 45-minute walk that hits a park, a church, and a university building.
What are some lesser-known places in the Upper West Side worth a visit?
